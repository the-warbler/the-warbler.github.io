Habits

Recycling

Recycle at least five recyclable items (e.g., plastic bottles, paper, glass) each day.

Reusable Bags

Remember to use reusable bags during every shopping trip. Check off each trip where you used them.

Compost Food Scraps

Add food scraps to your compost bin each day. Aim for at least one cup of scraps daily.

Plant one tree

Commit to planting at least one tree or plant each month and track it.

Walking or Biking

Walk or bike for at least one short trip (under 1 mile) each day and track it.

Take Shorter Showers

Limit showers to 5-10 minutes daily.
